Projects we support

Our organisation is committed to supporting registered non-governmental and volunteer organisations in Ukraine that work towards the betterment of civilians. We focus on aiding children, young people, women, and vulnerable individuals. Given the ongoing humanitarian crisis brought about by the Russian invasion, our current focus is on raising funds for emergency medical supplies for hospitals throughout Ukraine. We are open to considering both small and large long-term projects, and we love partnering with other organisations to co-finance projects in Ukraine. Our mission is to help ease the suffering of those impacted by the harsh life circumstances or the war unleashed by Russia against Ukraine.

How to apply for help

If you want to collaborate with us and apply for grants from Ukraine Charity, you need to fill out the application form and submit it. Once we receive your application, we will review it carefully. If your project meets all our requirements for funding, we will get in touch with you as soon as possible.
